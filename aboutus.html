About Us

Welcome to Shree Jain Traders, your reliable destination for all your electronic needs. With a legacy that dates back to 1968, we have been at the forefront of technological advancements, serving customers with excellence and providing innovative solutions for over five decades. Established during an era of rapid technological progress, Shree Jain Traders was founded by a team of passionate electronics enthusiasts who recognized the vast potential of the emerging field. Our founders' unwavering commitment to innovation and dedication to customer satisfaction laid the foundation for what would become a renowned electronics store. Throughout the years, Shree Jain Traders has grown and evolved, adapting to the dynamic landscape of the electronics industry. From the early days of vacuum tubes and analog systems to the digital revolution and the rise of smart devices, we have embraced every technological breakthrough and remained at the forefront of change. At Shree Jain Traders, we take immense pride in offering a diverse range of high-quality electronic products that cater to the needs of our esteemed customers. Whether you are a technology enthusiast, a professional seeking reliable equipment, or a hobbyist exploring new possibilities, our extensive inventory has something for everyone. Our knowledgeable and dedicated team of experts stands ready to assist you. With their deep understanding of the latest trends and advancements, they provide personalized recommendations and technical support, ensuring that you make informed decisions and get the most out of your purchases. Customer satisfaction has always been the driving force behind our success. We strive to create a seamless shopping experience, both in-store and online, by offering competitive prices, hassle-free returns, and exceptional customer service. Our unwavering commitment to excellence has earned us the trust and loyalty of countless customers throughout the years. In addition to our retail operations, Shree Jain Traders has forged strong partnerships with leading electronics manufacturers and suppliers. These collaborations enable us to bring you the latest products from renowned brands, guaranteeing that you have access to the most innovative and reliable technologies available. As we step into a new era of electronics, characterized by artificial intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), and sustainable solutions, Shree Jain Traders remains steadfast in staying ahead of the curve. We continuously explore emerging trends, expand our product offerings, and invest in research and development to anticipate the needs of our customers and provide them with the best options. We are proud of our heritage and excited about the future. At Shree Jain Traders, we are more than just an electronics store; we are a community of technology enthusiasts, professionals, and innovators who share a passion for all things electronic. Join us on this extraordinary journey as we continue to shape the world of technology together. We look forward to serving you and being your trusted partner in all your electronic endeavors.

Leadership

Late Mr.Mishrimal Jain

Founder and Chairman

“Bhaisa” as Mr. M.b.Jain is fondly known, is the brain behind the electronics superstore,Shree Jain Traders. With a bare minimum capital of Rs.10,000 and a heart filled with courage, Mr.M Jain began a journey to build an empire, brick by brick. Unlike his other Marwari friends and relatives who majorly chose Jewellery or Moneylending as their profession,Young enthusiast, he shared the dream of opening an electronic store which would give choice, comfort, and convenience to the customers.It was his sheer determination to succeed and devotion towards his guests that made a small store in Naupada a household name. In time, the stores multiplied and transformed Shree Jain Traders into one of the most successful electronics retail chain in Thane. Like a true visionary, Mr.M Jain has the phenomenal ability to understand consumer behaviour. This skill, along with his bold decision-taking abilities form the very foundation of Shree Jain Traders success story that has given the citizens of Thane a perfect place to shop for happiness.

Mr.Suresh Jain

Managing Director

Being from a business family,business mindset was always in blood of Suresh which made him join the venture at the age of 16years alongwith managing his studies.His detailed understanding about product technicalities and his approach towards the guests made sure that he upheld the Company’s long-standing philosophy of serving the guests with the best in electronics and services.Although difficult but he successfully managed to change the traditional approach of family to operate from single branch to expanding it to 3 branches.And till date is thickly involved in the running of the business as well as mentoring the next generation on various aspects.
